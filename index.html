"The work is serious. The man behind it is something else."

Cliff Schwandner has been a professional fine artist for over twenty years. Working primarily in acrylic and mixed media, his paintings move boldly between abstraction and figuration — large canvases filled with color, tension, and unmistakable point of view.

His work has been featured in galleries across the country and earned recognition along the way. Each painting begins as a question and ends somewhere unexpected.
